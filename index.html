About Us

Our main priority is to enlighten the public on the basic principles of Buddhism: wisdom and peace.

We believe that a healthy mind and body is a freedome every individual should enjoy. We intend on spreading this message through fresh, affordable Vietnamese Vegetarian and vegan cuisine. Food unites people across the globe and web hope to accomplish that goal here in Texas.

We hope you enjoy our food.

Our Team

We are a group of Buddhist nuns and volunteers that work and live at a local temple in Manor, Texas. There, we cook and prep veggitarian food for our guests for our daily ceremonies. We then decided to share our food with the general public to further spread awareness of the health benifits of a vegitarian lifestyle.
